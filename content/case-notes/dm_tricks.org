OOC, DM tricks stolen from Sam


- Temporary consequences like capture are interesting.
- Play with information.
- Change the definition of success on a roll.
- Time, minigames, etc.
- Have secondary objectives and clear fallbacks.
- Make failure a serious possibility.
- Drown people in leads
- Impose time limits on players if you have to
